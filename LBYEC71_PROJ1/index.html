MESSAGE TO THE READER...

Books, no matter how thick or thin they are, they contain stories that compel, excite, frustrate, react, and make us think. In my opinion, the true effect of books as an art form is in the fictional genre. It's quite amazing that more fictional books are made each year as this shows to us readers and non-readers alike that man's ideas are endless and have the ability to affect and touch our whole society. This is why I have created this Top 5 fiction books of all time in my opinion. Enjoy!

TOP 5 FICTIONAL BOOKS TO READ

  1. It Ends With Us (by: Colleen Hoover)

    2. “It stops here. With me and you. It ends with us.”

    It Ends With Us is a novel by New York Times bestselling author Colleen Hoover. In this novel, it points out that pain sometimes comes from the person who is closest to us or who we love most.

    Will you choose the past or the present?

    Watch out. This book will leave you speechless and thinking for days or more.

  2. Shatter Me (Series) (by: Tahereh Mafi)

    3. “I'm oxygen and he's dying to breathe.”

    Shatter Me is a series written by author Tahereh Mafi. It has a total of four books (and counting):

    1. Shatter Me
    2. Unravel Me
    3. Ignite Me
    4. Restore Me (newly published)
    5. (Book 5 to be published)

    This novel is about Juliette, a girl whose touch is lethal and can kill or paralyze anyone she touches, yet powerful enough to save the world. There are great twists and events in this book which will make you say "WTH?" and "OMG!" Shatter Me is a fresh and original dystopian novel with a paranormal twist that will leave readers anxiously awaiting its sequel. So what happens if Juliette is set free and exposed to the world? Will her touch bring damage or will it bring safety?

  3. Every Day (by: David Levithan)

    4. “It's as if when you love someone, they become your reason.”

    Every Day is a New York Times Bestseller young adult romance and fantasy novel written by author David Levithan. It is about the story of A, a person who wakes up in a different body each day. Always the same age, but never the same person twice. A does not have a real name, presumably because they don't really exist physically, but is a whole person emotionally and intelectually. So what happens when A meets a girl who is the girlfriend of Justin, the body that A occupied for a day?

  4. Beautiful Disaster (by: Jamie McGuire)

    5. INTENSE. DANGEROUS. ADDICTIVE.

    Beautiful Disaster is an adult novel by American author Jamie McGuire. This book is the typical good-girl-bad-boy-fell-in-love-with-each-other story. But there are twists and events that you would not expect from the story which makes it good and interesting.

  5. Anna Dressed In Blood (by: Kendare Blake)

    6. "Anna, my strong, terrifying Anna."

    In a world of ghosts and ghost hunters, Cas Lowood, a teenager with a knack for killing ghosts finds himself in a new town where he meets his match: A dead, slavic girl that haunts a house where people seemed to have disappeared. Little did he know that they would fall so intimately for each other amidst troubled times, dangerous times where the hunter can also be the hunted.
